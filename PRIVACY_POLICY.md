# Privacy Policy - ScoreboardEssential

**Last updated:** January 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ScoreboardEssential ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and its companion Wear OS application (collectively, the "App").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Local Data Storage (Room Database)
The App stores match data, including scores, team names, player statistics (goals, appearances), and match history, locally on your device using an internal database (Room). This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers owned or operated by us.

### Wearable Data Layer
The App communicates with your paired Wear OS device to synchronize match data (scores, timer, team names) in real-time. This communication occurs directly between your devices using the Google Play Services Wearable Data Layer API. No data is sent to the cloud or third-party servers during this process.

### Storage/Media Access
The App requests `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` permission (on devices running Android 9 and below) to allow you to save or export match reports and data locally. On newer Android versions, the App utilizes scoped storage to save files securely without requiring broad storage permissions. We only access files created by the App.

### Camera Usage
The App does **not** access your device's camera or microphone.

## 2. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. All data generated by the App is stored locally on your device or synchronized between your personal devices.

## 3. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the data stored locally on your device. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
